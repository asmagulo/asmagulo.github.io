Ainur Smagulova

I'm a third year Computer Science PhD student at the University of California San Diego. I'm working in the Database Group with professor Alin Deutsch.

My research interests lie at the intersection of the theory and applications of databases and big data management systems. Currently, I'm focused on the topic of data processing for massively parallel systems: parallel computation of queries over graphs and its complexity.
